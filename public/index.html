Pig Sticker + Akarat's Awakening + cubed Blood Brother (or Furnace). Very solid option. Provoke can either stay or can be changed to Consecration - Bed of Nails. But it's very hard to get a good Akarat's Awakening with block chance, cdr and thorns. Pig Sticker is worse than Hack. You will notice difference when you try both. Advantages of Pig Sticker are: higher base APS (attacks per second), because it's a dagger and not axe, like Hack.. and also it rolls wtih 5 stats instead of 4, so, potentially you can get all stats you need, like LOH, Area Damage, Attack Speed and reroll base damage to CDR, for example. Overall Pig Sticker is a solid option, unless you find your "perfect" Hack
